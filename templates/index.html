Radio Anything

What

This is an app that returns a random internet station and plays it

Why

I listen to music and podcasts all the time when I work. The issue is, because I consume so much of it, the algorithm has overfitted itself. I find myself listening to the same DJ set from Ultra or band at Coachella over and over again, simply because nothing new is showing up and i cant be arsed to search. With this, I have no control over what it decides to play me. If I like what its playing, it can continue, but if I don't, it's on to the next one.
